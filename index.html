Tic Tac Toe Game For 2

Rules of the game

The game is played on a grid that's 5 squares by 5 squares.

The mark of the player 1 is X and that of the player 2 is O.

The first player to get 5 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally or diagonally) is the winner.

If all 25 squares get full, it's a tie.

Good luck!
